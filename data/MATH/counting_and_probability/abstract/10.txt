Problem: Compute $\dbinom{\alpha}{\beta}$.

Solution: \begin{align*}
\dbinom{\alpha}{\beta} &= \dfrac{\alpha !}{\beta!(\alpha-\beta)!} \\
&= \dfrac{\alpha\times(\alpha-1)\times\cdots\times(\beta+1)}{2\times 3\times\cdots\times(\alpha-\beta)} \\
&= \boxed{\gamma}.
\end{align*}

Final Answer: \gamma
