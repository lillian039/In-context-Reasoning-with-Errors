Level: Level 1

Problem: Compute $\dbinom{b}{a}$.

Solution: \begin{align*}
\dbinom{b}{a} &= \dfrac{b!}{a!a!} \\
&= \dfrac{b\times d\times e\times f}{a\times g\times h\times k} \\
&= \dfrac{b}{a\times h\times k}\times d \times \dfrac{e}{g} \times f \\
&= k\times d\times h\times f \\
&= \boxed{c}.
\end{align*}

Final Answer: c
