Problem: What is the value of $$\frac{m}{l}\times k\times\frac{m}{h}\times g\times\frac{m}{f}\times e\times\frac{m}{d}\times c\times\frac{m}{b}\times a?$$

Solution: We pair up the terms in the product as follows: $$
\left(\frac{m}{l} \times k\right) \times \left(\frac{m}{h} \times g\right) \times \left(\frac{m}{f} \times e\right) \times \left(\frac{m}{d} \times c\right) \times \left(\frac{m}{b} \times a\right).
$$The value inside each pair of parentheses is $l,$ so the answer is $l \times l \times l \times l \times l = \boxed{f}.$

Final Answer: f
