Problem: What is the value of $$a \times b\times c\times d\times e\times f\times g\times h\times i\times j?$$

Solution: We pair up the terms in the product as follows: $$
\left(a \times b\right) \times \left(c \times d\right) \times \left(e \times f\right) \times \left(g \times h\right) \times \left(i \times j\right).
$$The value inside each pair of parentheses is $\alpha,$ so the answer is $\alpha \times \alpha \times \alpha \times \alpha \times \alpha = \boxed{\beta}.$

Final Answer: \beta
