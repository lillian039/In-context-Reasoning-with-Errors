Problem: Trevor buys several bouquets of carnations. The first included 9 carnations; the second included 14 carnations; the third included 13 carnations. What is the average number of carnations in the bouquets?

Solution: The sum is 9+14+13=<<9+14+13=36>>36.
There are 3 bouquets.
The average is 36/3=<<36/3=12>>12.

Final Answer: 12
