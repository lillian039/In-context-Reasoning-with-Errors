Problem: The state of Virginia had 3.79 inches of rain in March, 4.5 inches of rain in April, 3.95 inches of rain in May, 3.09 inches of rain in June and 4.67 inches in July.  What is the average rainfall amount, in inches, in Virginia?

Solution: It rained for a total of 3.79+4.5+3.95+3.09+4.67 = <<3.79+4.5+3.95+3.09+4.67=25>>25 inches
The rain period is from March through July for a total of 5 months so the average rainfall is 25/5 = <<25/5=5>>5 inches of rain per month

Final Answer: 5
