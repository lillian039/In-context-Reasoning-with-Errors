Problem: Kyle bought 2 glass bottles that can hold 15 origami stars each. He then bought another 3 identical glass bottles. How many stars must Kyle make to fill all the glass bottles he bought?

Solution: Kyle has 2 + 3 = <<2+3=5>>5 glass bottles.
He needs to make 15 x 5 = <<15*5=75>>75 origami stars

Final Answer: 75
