Problem: The state of Virginia had 3.79 inches of rain in March, 4.5 inches of rain in April, 3.95 inches of rain in May, 3.09 inches of rain in June and 4.67 inches in July.  What is the average rainfall amount, in inches, in Virginia?

Solution: How many inches did it rain in Virginia? ** It rained for a total of 3.79+4.5+3.95+3.09+4.67 = <<3.79+4.5+3.95+3.09+4.67=20>>20 inches
How many inches of rain did it rain in Virginia? ** The rain period is from March through July for a total of 5 months so the average rainfall is 20/5 = <<20/5=4>>4 inches of rain per month

Final Answer: 4
